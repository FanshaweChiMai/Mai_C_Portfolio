About Chi
I am no uniquely creative designer. Being born in Saigon, Vietnam but I grew up to be fascinated by the diversity of world culture. After having obtained a Bachelor Degree of Fine Arts in Interior Design in United States, I have worked in construction, branding, and service design moving step by step to bring culturally and traditionally inspired design with in-depth concerns for the environment. I enjoy arts in many forms, and tolerate no destruction.Complications back in homeland brought me to Canada and interactive media design field. Feel free to take a look at my completed works, and drop a hello at mai.chi@chimai.ca. My social media profiles below are always open to new friendship.
