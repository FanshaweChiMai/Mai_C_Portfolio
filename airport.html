ROLE

CLIENT
college project

Tan Son Nhut International Airport project was part of my institutional design project during my years in Interior design at the Art Institute of Houston in which I imagined an environmental friendly and human wellness considered lounge for passengers who have to stay on long flights and wait long transit hours. The inspiration comes from personal experiences of flying up to 30 hours multiple times between Vietnam and the States. The project was sketched in AutoCAD, built and rendered in 3ds max.
