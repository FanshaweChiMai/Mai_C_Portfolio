Project Info

ROLE
interior design

CLIENT
Dong Do Hotel Investors

Conceptual design for Dong Do Hotel during year 2015 as requested for a renovation for an old building. The hotel consists of Japanese and contemporary elements due to the fact that the investors are from Japan and the hotel is located in Saigon, Vietnam where contemporary interior design is the trend. The project was sketched in autoCAD, built in SketchUp, and rendered in 3dsmax.
